Who is The Scent Queen?

Specialists in Wax Melts and Car Scents, we'll make both your car and home smell amazing!

So here I thought I’d tell you a little bit about behind the scenes of the scent queen_. The scent queen_ is a small wax melt company run by myself in West Sussex. I’ve always had a love for wax melts. I’ve always thought about starting to make them but never got round to it. I have my own cleaning company so was always pretty busy with that.

Then this horrible corona virus happened and I was unable to work, I thought what perfect timing to start a company like this where I can work from home. So I decided to start up a sister company to my cleaning business and work them alongside each other. My future plan is to expand my business by adding new products.
